Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At The Reading Capsule, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses for newsletter subscriptions, names when provided via contact forms
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for analytics and security purposes

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

  • To deliver our newsletter and personalized content
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure servers with encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Email delivery (Mailchimp, etc.)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Payment processing (for any future premium services)

These services have their own privacy policies which we recommend you review.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of communications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated to subscribers.

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through our Contact Page.
