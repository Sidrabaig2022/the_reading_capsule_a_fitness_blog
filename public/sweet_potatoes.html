Sweet Potatoes: America's Superfood

Packed with nutrients and health benefits for your whole family

Sweet potatoes are one of nature's most nutritious and versatile superfoods. These vibrant root vegetables offer numerous health benefits that make them a valuable addition to any American diet.

Nutritional Powerhouse (1 medium baked with skin)

Essential Vitamins

  • Vitamin A: 438% DV
  • Vitamin C: 37% DV
  • Vitamin B6: 16% DV

Macronutrients

  • Calories: 103
  • Carbs: 24g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Protein: 2g

Minerals

  • Manganese: 28% DV
  • Potassium: 15% DV
  • Magnesium: 8% DV

Best Ways to Enjoy Sweet Potatoes

Baked or Roasted

Preserves nutrients better than boiling. Try with cinnamon for extra antioxidants.

Air-Fried Fries

Healthier alternative to regular fries with more fiber and nutrients.

Mashed

Creamy comfort food that pairs well with lean proteins.

In Smoothies

Adds natural sweetness and creaminess to breakfast shakes.
