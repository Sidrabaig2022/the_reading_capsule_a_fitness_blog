⚽ Arsenal vs Real Madrid – Champions League QF Preview

The UEFA Champions League quarter-final first leg between Arsenal and Real Madrid is set to take place on April 8, 2025, at the Emirates Stadium in London. This highly anticipated clash features Arsenal, aiming for their first European Cup title, against Real Madrid, the competition's most successful club with 15 titles.

🔍 Team News and Line-ups

🟥 Arsenal

Bukayo Saka returns to the starting lineup after recovering from surgery, marking his first appearance in 2025. Jakub Kiwior replaces the injured Gabriel Magalhaes in defense.

  • Goalkeeper: David Raya
  • Defenders: Jurriën Timber, William Saliba, Jakub Kiwior, Myles Lewis-Skelly
  • Midfielders: Martin Ødegaard, Thomas Partey, Declan Rice
  • Forwards: Bukayo Saka, Mikel Merino, Gabriel Martinelli
⚪ Real Madrid

Thibaut Courtois returns from injury to start in goal. Eduardo Camavinga and David Alaba feature in defense due to injuries and suspensions.

  • Goalkeeper: Thibaut Courtois
  • Defenders: Fede Valverde, Raúl Asencio, David Alaba, Eduardo Camavinga
  • Midfielders: Luka Modrić, Federico Valverde, Jude Bellingham
  • Forwards: Vinícius Júnior, Kylian Mbappé, Rodrygo

📜 Historical Context

This is only the second time Arsenal and Real Madrid face off competitively in the Champions League. Their previous meeting was in the 2005-06 Round of 16, where Arsenal emerged victorious.

🎯 Match Significance

Arsenal hopes to take full advantage of their home turf before the second leg in Madrid. Real Madrid, with their rich UCL experience, looks to take control of the tie with a solid away performance.

🕒 Kick-off Details

  • Date: April 8, 2025
  • Time: 8:00 PM BST (12:30 AM IST on April 9, 2025)
  • Venue: Emirates Stadium, London

📺 Viewing Information

Fans can watch the match live through UEFA Champions League official broadcasters in their respective countries.
