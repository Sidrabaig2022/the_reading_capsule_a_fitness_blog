15 Killer Ab Workouts to Sculpt Your Core

No Gym Required!

Looking for the ultimate list of ab workouts that actually work? Whether you're on a weight loss journey, toning up for summer, or just want a stronger core, these 15 ab exercises will fire up your midsection and get you closer to those dream abs. No equipment. No gym. Just you and your mat!

Why Focus on Abs?

Strong abdominal muscles improve posture, balance, and overall body strength. This 15-day ab workout is perfect for beginners and pros alike. Follow along with daily workouts and animated demos.

Day 1

Crunches

The classic move to target your upper abs.


How to do it: Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat. Lift your upper body off the floor.

Reps: 3 sets of 20

Day 2

2. Bicycle Crunches

Engage both upper and lower abs + obliques.


How to do it: Pedal your legs in the air while touching opposite elbow to knee.

Reps: 3 sets of 15 each side

Day 3

3. Russian Twists

Perfect for trimming your waist.


How to do it: Sit, lean back slightly, twist your torso side to side. Optional: Hold a water bottle or dumbbell.

Reps: 3 sets of 30 (15 each side)

Day 4

4. Leg Raises

Targets those stubborn lower abs.


How to do it: Lie down, raise legs straight up, then lower without touching the ground.

Reps: 3 sets of 12

Day 5

5. Plank

A full-core burner!


How to do it: Forearms down, hold your body in a straight line.

Time: 3 rounds of 30â€“60 seconds

Day 6

6. Mountain Climbers

Cardio + abs in one move.


How to do it: In a plank, run your knees toward your chest fast.

Reps: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Day 7

7. V-Ups

Advanced move for upper & lower abs.


How to do it: Lie flat, then lift arms and legs into a V shape. Reps: 3 sets of 10

Day 8

8. Flutter Kicks

Lower ab isolation at its finest.


How to do it: Lie down, lift legs slightly, and kick them up and down alternately. Reps: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Day 9

9. Heel Touches

Simple but highly effective for your side abs.


How to do it: Lie on your back, knees bent. Reach side to side touching heels. Reps: 3 sets of 20

Day 10

10. Reverse Crunches

Take pressure off your neck and hit the lower core.


How to do it: Lift hips off the ground using your lower abs. Reps: 3 sets of 15

Day 11

11. Side Plank (With Hip Dips)

Obliques on fire!


How to do it: In a side plank, dip your hips toward the floor then lift. Reps: 3 sets of 10 per side

Day 12

12. Dead Bug

Great for engaging the deep core muscles.


How to do it: On your back, arms and legs up. Lower opposite arm and leg together. Reps: 3 sets of 10 per side

Day 13

13. Toe Touches

Burns the upper core and stretches your hamstrings.


How to do it: Lie down, legs up, reach your hands toward your toes. Reps: 3 sets of 15

Day 14

14. Scissor Kicks

Core tightening move that hits everything.


How to do it: Lie flat, lift legs and alternate crossing them. Reps: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Day 15

15. Standing Oblique Crunch

Great for those who hate floor workouts.


How to do it: Stand up, bring knee up to meet elbow side to side. Reps: 3 sets of 15 per side

Keep going! Repeat similar intensity, adding reps as you build strength throughout the month.
