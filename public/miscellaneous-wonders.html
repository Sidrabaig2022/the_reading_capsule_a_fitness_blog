Did you know that honey never spoils? Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.
The longest hiccuping spree lasted 68 years! Charles Osborne started hiccupping in 1922 and continued until 1990.
The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar on August 27, 1896. Zanzibar surrendered after just 38 minutes.
Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't. Botanically, a berry has seeds and pulp produced from a single ovary, which bananas fit and strawberries do not.
Wombat poop is cube-shaped, which helps it stay in place and not roll away, marking their territory efficiently.
The inventor of the Pringles can is now buried in one. Fred Baur, who invented the can, had his ashes partially buried in one after his death in 2008.
Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.
A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes Venus about 243 Earth days to rotate once, but only about 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.
Sloths can hold their breath longer than dolphins can. While dolphins need to surface roughly every 10 minutes, sloths can hold their breath for up to 40 minutes.
There's a species of jellyfish known as Turritopsis dohrnii, nicknamed the "immortal jellyfish," because it can revert to its juvenile form and potentially live forever.
