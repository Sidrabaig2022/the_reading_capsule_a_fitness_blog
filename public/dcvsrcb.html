RCB vs DC - IPL 2025 Match Preview

The Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025 season is heating up as the Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) prepare to host the Delhi Capitals (DC) on April 10, 2025, at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru. This highly anticipated match features two of the top-performing teams this season, both vying for supremacy in the league standings.

Match Details:

  • Date: April 10, 2025
  • Time: 7:30 PM IST
  • Venue: M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru

Team Form and Standings:

Delhi Capitals remain unbeaten with three consecutive wins under Axar Patel. RCB, led by Rajat Patidar, has won three out of four matches, reflecting a strong start.

Head-to-Head Record:

  • Total Matches Played: 31
  • RCB Wins: 19
  • DC Wins: 11
  • No Result: 1

At the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium:

Pitch and Weather Conditions:

The pitch is batting-friendly, but a green tinge could assist fast bowlers. Evening conditions are expected to be cloudy with potential showers.
