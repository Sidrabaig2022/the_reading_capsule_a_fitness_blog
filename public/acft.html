What's New in the 2025 ACFT?

The 2025 ACFT maintains the same 6-event structure but introduces updated scoring benchmarks based on 3 years of Army-wide data:

  • Gender-neutral scoring for plank event
  • Adjusted weight standards for deadlift
  • New age-group modifiers for reservists

2025 ACFT FAQs

Are there different standards for National Guard/Reserve in 2025?

Yes. The 2025 ACFT introduces age-adjusted standards for reservists over 40, with modified requirements for deadlift and run events.

Has the plank time requirement changed?

The 2025 standards maintain the 1:05-3:30 minute range but now allow a 5-second rest position (knees down) for soldiers over 50.
